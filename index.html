Scientific Overview

The Solar System consists of the Sun and everything bound to it by gravity - eight planets, five dwarf planets, hundreds of moons, and millions of asteroids, comets, and meteoroids. It formed approximately 4.6 billion years ago from the gravitational collapse of a giant interstellar molecular cloud.
